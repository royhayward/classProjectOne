THE FIRST PERSON SHOOTER(fps)

A first person shooter is just that, run around in a first person POV and shoot things.

On this page I'm going to review a few FPS games for you.

Krunker.IO

The first FPS game on my list is quite popular in schools and is called Krunker.IO I played this game for about half an hour and I am not the best but im ok this is a game that will definetly keep you on your toes because the maps are relatively small wich makes for an interesting play experience where you always go around a corner expecting to shoot someone on the othe side and stanting still is the worst thing you can do. I love this games simplistic graphics i give it a 8/10

CONTRACT WARS

next up we have Contract Wars i played this game for 30-50 min and for the first half of it i couldnt get anything done cause i was distracted by the amazing graphics, UI and visuals similar to other FPS games i play standing still is an absolutely horroble idea. great graphics good gameplay I give this game a 9.5/10
